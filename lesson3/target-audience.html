Target Audience

The target audience for this website are people who are interested in information about the local community. People may be interested in information about jobs that they want to work in, or information about service projects that they want to do. They may also want to learn general information about the community such as tourist locations.

Personas

Persona 1

  • Group: Job Seeker
  • Fictional Name: Billy Joe
  • Job Title: Unemployed
  • Demographics: Married, 2 children, third job
  • Goals and Tasks: Ready and willing to work, needs a good job to provide for his family.
  • Environment: Comfortable with high workloads and longer shifts. Worked as a mechanic and a construction worker.
  • Quote: "I was wondering if there were any recommendations you had for jobs available."

Persona 2

  • Group: Tourist
  • Fictional Name: Frank Smith
  • Job Title: Firefighter
  • Demographics: Married, 4 kids, retired
  • Goals and Tasks: Wants to see what sights can be seen locally.
  • Environment: On vacation for a while and wants to find places to relax and have fun sightseeing.
  • Quote: "I am looking for places nearby that we can go sightseeing, do you know of any places nearby?"

Scenarios

  • I am in town for a couple of weeks, what would be the best things to do while I am here?
  • I just lost my job and am looking for another one nearby, are there any jobs available?
  • I don't like my current job, can you recommend another one that is more suited to my career of choice?
  • Are there any homeless shelters nearby that are in need of donations, or service?
  • What options nearby do we have for community service?
