New Mexico Tech

The school of mines. Best known for the study of geology and engineering. Founded in the mid 1800s, this school has even been visited by the Mythbusters, Jamie and Adam!

Trinity Site

The trinity site is known as the site of the first atomic bomb explosion. Located south of Socorro, this site opens twice a year for tourists to view the aftermath of the world's first nuclear explosion. Today, there are only slight traces of radiation, but a small still crater remains.

VLA

The VLA is home to one of the most sophisticated radio telescope arrays ever constructed. The radio telescopes monitor and construct images of deep space from recieved radio waves.
